Rockt is a geopresence game that takes place on Toronto's streetcar network. Every time you ride a streetcar, you can check-in, and check out every time you leave. As you ride, you will be able to purchase your cars, and levy fees on others. Amass a fleet and control Toronto! Can you run your network better than the TTC can?

Rockt is currently in private beta. To request an invitation, please register.
